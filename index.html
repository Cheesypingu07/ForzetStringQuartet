About Us

Forzet String Quartet is sure to make your special day more memorable! With their talent and affordable prices, these coastal bend musicians are fast becoming a must-have at weddings, business events, banquets, and private parties. Boasting an expansive and ever-growing catalog of over 250 songs, Forzet will work with you to curate a playlist most suited to your event’s needs. The elegance of their string music will surely make your event an unforgettable experience. Hire them today starting at just $450/hour, and schedule your free consultation to learn more.
